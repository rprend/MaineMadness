About Maine Madness

Welcome to Maine South's March Madness website!

Engage in some friendly competition for a good cause! After you complete your bracket, please pay $5 in the bookstore to enter the competition; all proceeds will go to the School Wide Fundraiser in support of finding a cure for Huntington's Disease. There will be prizes for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place in both individual and class categories, as well as a prize for Biggest Loser. Prizes include apparel from the college of your choice or free breakfast/pizza for the winning class. Join the tournament today!

Please note that if you do not pay $5 in the bookstore, your account will be deleted when the torunament starts.

You will be able to create your bracket as soon as the teams are released!
